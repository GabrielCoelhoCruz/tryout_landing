## Codebase Patterns
- Landing page is a single-file component at src/app/page.tsx with inline sections
- Constants/data in src/constants/landing-data.ts
- ScrollProgress component at src/components/ui/ScrollProgress.tsx (side dots + top bar)
- Footer is inline in page.tsx (landing) and also exists as src/components/Footer.tsx (used by form page)
- Navbar is z-50 fixed position
- All animations use framer-motion
- Font: Bebas Neue (display) + Work Sans (body)
